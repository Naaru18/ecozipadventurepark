Our Story

From a Forest Trail to AP's #1 Park

It started with a rope and a bad idea. In 2018, Ravi Kumar — a forestry graduate from Nellore — strung a 50-metre zipline between two mango trees behind his father's land. He charged ₹20 a ride. It was rickety, probably illegal, and absolutely exhilarating.

One year later, EcoZip Adventures opened as a fully certified park on 18 acres of private forest reserve — the first ACCT-accredited adventure course in Andhra Pradesh. What began as a backyard experiment became the benchmark for outdoor adventure in coastal AP.

Today, EcoZip runs 12 distinct activities, employs 34 people from surrounding villages, and has hosted over 8,000 adventurers — from schoolkids on their first outdoor trip to corporate teams from Hyderabad looking for something beyond a conference room.

What started as a small trail-side venture has grown into 12 unique adventure activities, a team of 34 local staff.

The rope got longer. The mission stayed the same.

What We Stand For

Mission & Core Values

Four principles that shape every rope we rig, every guide we hire, and every tree we plant.

Safety is Non-Negotiable

We treat safety not as a checklist but as a culture. Every team member — from guides to maintenance crew — is trained to stop operations the moment anything looks wrong. No ride is worth a compromise.

"When in doubt, we don't."
Eco-Responsibility

The forest is our home and our product. We operate carbon-neutral, ban single-use plastics site-wide, and actively partner with conservation organisations to grow the forest around us — not just preserve it.

"Leave it better than you found it."
Community First

94% of our workforce comes from villages within 15 km of the park. We run free adventure days for local schools twice a year and channel a portion of every ticket into the Nellore Forest Education Fund.

"Adventure should lift everyone around it."
Thrill Without Compromise

We refuse to dumb down our experiences. The ziplines are genuinely fast, the climbs are genuinely hard, and the campsites are genuinely wild. We just make sure every bit of that thrill is delivered safely.

"Real adventure. Real safety. No contradiction."
