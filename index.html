Common Questions

Anything Unclear?

What are the age and weight limits?
Most activities require a minimum age of 10 and a maximum weight of 120 kg. The Dual Zipline requires age 10+. Rappelling and Night Zip require age 12+. Children under 16 must be accompanied by a guardian.
What should I wear and bring?
Wear closed-toe shoes (mandatory), comfortable athletic clothing, and avoid loose jewellery. Bring a light jacket for the canopy — it can get breezy. We provide all safety equipment. Lockers are available for bags.
What is your weather cancellation policy?
We monitor weather continuously. If we cancel due to unsafe conditions, you receive a full refund or free rescheduling. If you cancel 48+ hours in advance, you receive a full refund. Cancellations under 24 hours receive a 50% refund.
Is prior experience required?
None at all. Every activity begins with a certified guide-led safety briefing and demonstration. We have thousands of first-timers every year — our guides are trained to ease nerves and ensure everyone feels confident before going.
Are there accessibility accommodations?
The Canopy Walk and Burma Bridge are accessible for most visitors. We assess each guest individually — please contact us in advance so we can prepare appropriately. We're committed to making adventure accessible to as many people as possible.
How far in advance should I book?
Weekends and holidays fill 2–3 weeks in advance. Weekdays are generally available with 1–2 days notice. Night Zipline slots are limited to 20 per session and sell out fastest — book as early as possible.
