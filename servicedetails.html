What to Expect

From Briefing to Landing

Every zipline session follows a structured 5-stage journey designed to build confidence before each line and maximize your experience throughout.

Stage 1 · 0 – 20 min
Arrival & Safety Briefing

Check in at base camp, meet your guide team, and attend a thorough 20-minute briefing covering all safety protocols, harness fitting, braking technique, and what to expect at each platform. No prior experience needed.

Stage 2 · 20 – 35 min
Gear Up & Practice Line

Get fitted with full-body harness, helmet, and gloves — all ISO-certified and inspected that morning. Run a short 80m practice line at low height to master your body position and landing posture before the real thing.

Stage 3 · 35 – 100 min
Lines 1–5: Forest Canopy Run

Traverse five progressively longer and faster lines through dense forest canopy, ranging from 180m to 520m. Guides stationed at every platform ensure seamless transitions. Height above ground varies from 12m to 35m.

Stage 4 · 100 – 130 min
Lines 6–7: The Big Finish

The final two lines are the longest and most thrilling: Line 6 crosses the valley at 45 metres — our highest point — and Line 7 is the legendary 680m finale at 60 km/h, ending at a hillside viewing platform.

Stage 5 · 130 – 150 min
Debrief & Refreshments

Return to base camp via a short forest walk. Complimentary herbal tea and snacks await. Guides share highlight photos taken during your run. Optional upgrade to get a full video package of your entire session.

Safety First

Built for Zero Compromise

Every system, every rope, every clip is inspected daily and meets international adventure course standards.

Dual-Clip Redundancy

Every rider is attached to the line with two independent clips. If one fails — which our equipment is engineered to prevent — the other holds. Industry-standard double-backup at all times.

ISO Certified Gear

All harnesses, helmets, and carabiners are CE-certified and replaced on schedule per manufacturer specification, not when they look worn.

Daily Inspections

Our technical team checks every line, anchor, and platform at 5:30 AM every day before the park opens. Inspection log is available at the front desk.

On-Site First Aid

A certified wilderness first responder is on site during all operational hours. Emergency response protocol drilled monthly with local Nellore EMS.

Weather Monitoring

Real-time wind speed and lightning monitoring. Courses close automatically when wind exceeds 40 km/h or lightning is detected within 15 km. Full refund on weather cancellations.

ACCT Accredited

EcoZip is accredited by the Association for Challenge Course Technology — the global gold standard for aerial adventure operations since our founding.

⚠️ Eligibility Requirements
Minimum age: 10 years · Minimum weight: 25 kg · Maximum weight: 120 kg · No participation if pregnant, cardiac conditions, recent surgeries, or severe vertigo. A waiver must be signed before participation. Minors require a parent/guardian signature.

Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Do I need any prior experience to zipline?+
Absolutely not. Our guides walk you through everything from harness fitting to braking technique during the 20-minute briefing. The practice line at the start means you'll be comfortable long before the real runs begin. First-timers make up over 70% of our riders.
What should I wear and bring?+
Wear comfortable, close-fitting clothing — nothing too loose that could catch. Closed-toe shoes are mandatory (no sandals). Avoid dangling jewellery. Bring sunscreen, a water bottle, and a light jacket for early morning sessions. Leave bags at base camp (lockers provided).
Is there a weight or age restriction?+
Participants must be at least 10 years old, weigh a minimum of 25 kg and no more than 120 kg. These limits are set by the harness manufacturer for your safety. Minors aged 10–17 require a signed guardian waiver.
Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?+
Yes. Free cancellation or rescheduling up to 48 hours before your slot. Within 48 hours, we issue a park credit valid for 6 months (no cash refunds). Weather cancellations initiated by us result in a full refund or free rescheduling — no questions asked.
Can I bring my phone or camera on the lines?+
Handheld cameras and loose phones are not permitted on the lines — they become dangerous projectiles at speed. We have GoPro mounts on select platforms and offer a professional video package (₹599) covering your entire session.
Do you offer group or corporate bookings?+
Yes — groups of 10 or more receive a 15% discount, and corporate packages (including team-building workshops, catering, and private park hours) are available on request. Email events@ecozipadventures.in or call us for a custom quote.
